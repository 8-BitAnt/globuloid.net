About Our Founder

Meet Kreg McPelivice, the driving force behind GlobuCorp. In 1992, Kreg started GlobuCorp in his garage with a vision to create a better future for people and globuloids alike. Today, GlobuCorp and its subsidiary, GlobuNation, have become the go-to place for glob-related supplies and information.

Kreg's passion for globuloids and his dedication to educating the world about them have shaped the success of GlobuCorp. With a deep understanding of globuloid behavior and needs, Kreg has pioneered innovative morphdablorf strategies and products that assist globuloid owners in providing optimal care. His expertise and guidance have helped countless individuals embark on their globuloid ownership journey with confidence.

Under Kreg's leadership, GlobuCorp has established itself as a trusted authority in the globuloid community. Through comprehensive educational resources, personalized consultations, and top-notch supplies, Kreg and his team continue to empower globuloid enthusiasts worldwide. He’s created a lasting legacy, revolutionizing the way we understand and interact with these extraordinary creatures.
